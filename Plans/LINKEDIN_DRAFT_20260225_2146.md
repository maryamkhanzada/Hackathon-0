# LinkedIn Draft: Education

## Post Text

Education is the highest-ROI investment any individual or organisation can make.

Here is what consistent learning has taught me:

**1. The best learners aren't the smartest — they're the most consistent.**
Small daily inputs beat weekend cramming every single time. 15 minutes a day adds up to 91 hours a year.

**2. Teaching others is the fastest way to master a skill yourself.**
If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it yet. Find someone to teach.

**3. Curiosity is a competitive advantage.**
In a world where information is free, the people who ask better questions win.

**4. The most in-demand skill in 2026?**
The ability to learn fast and apply faster. Not a degree — a habit.

Whether you're building a team or building yourself: invest in education first. Everything else follows.
